<p>Following on from the recent Gartner Magic Quadrant Report on SAM Tool’s 2020, we were left baffled by the section on Certero. Not only was it factually incorrect in many places, but it left me wondering what impact this could have on many of the organizations who use this report to find a new SAM partner.</p>



<p>However to help, I thought it would be good to go through the actual detail of the report and highlight where Gartner got it wrong, but also where they were right (spoiler alert, it wasn’t many!).</p>



<p>So, to help, I have created a fact vs fiction that hopefully helps clarify the real truth about Certero:</p>



<p>1.&nbsp;<strong>What Gartner said about Certero<em>:&nbsp;</em></strong>Certero provides a platform for both hardware asset management (HAM) and SAM&nbsp;</p>



<p><em><strong>Certero says:</strong>&nbsp;Yes, this is&nbsp;correct.&nbsp;It’s a true, unified platform that offers a single source of normalized data, straight out of the box. Meaning our customers can interrogate their most valuable assets and manage all their hardware, software assets, that reside on-premises or in their private/public cloud. All with full functionality that can be delivered as SaaS, on-premises or as a hybrid solution. </em></p>



<p>2.&nbsp;<strong>What Gartner said about Certero<em>:&nbsp;</em></strong>&nbsp;Headquartered in Warrington, UK&nbsp;</p>



<p><em><strong>Certero says:&nbsp;</strong>Certero’s UK business has an office in Warrington, UK. Its America business has an office&nbsp;in&nbsp;Chicago and its business in Australia has an office in Sydney. (See our locations&nbsp;<a href="https://www.certero.com/locations/">here</a><a href="https://www.certero.com/locations/">)</a></em></p>



<p>3.&nbsp;<strong>What Gartner said about Certero<em>:&nbsp;</em></strong>Certero operates largely within Europe, specializing in IT asset management (ITAM) and SAM tool and Services&nbsp;</p>



<p><strong><em>Certero says:&nbsp;</em></strong><em>Certero has more customers in Malaysia than it does in Europe. Certero has more customers in North America than it does in Europe. Certero has more customers in Australia and New Zealand than it does in Europe.&nbsp;</em></p>



<p>4.&nbsp;<strong>What Gartner said about Certero<em>:&nbsp;</em></strong>Certero Asset Studio for Enterprise SAM is complimented by additional modules, which include App-Centre, its self-service portal, as well as four modules for Oracle, IBM, SAP and cloud for enhanced optimization capabilities.&nbsp;</p>



<p><em><strong>Certero says:&nbsp;</strong>Certero&nbsp;have&nbsp;the following Products:</em></p>



<p><em><strong>1.&nbsp;</strong></em><em><strong><a href="https://www.certero.com/itam/">Certero for Enterprise ITAM&nbsp;</a></strong></em><em>product has four&nbsp;optional&nbsp;modules that cover Inventory, Monitoring, Distribution and Patching&nbsp;</em></p>



<p><em><strong>2.&nbsp;<a href="https://www.certero.com/sam/">Certero for Enterprise SAM</a>&nbsp;</strong>product has four&nbsp;optional&nbsp;modules that cover Generic Licensing, Microsoft, Adobe,&nbsp;AccessCtrl&nbsp;</em></p>



<p><em><strong>3.&nbsp;Certero for Oracle&nbsp;</strong>product has two&nbsp;optional&nbsp;modules, one&nbsp;that covers&nbsp;Database, Middleware&nbsp;and the other module covers&nbsp;E-Business Suite&nbsp;</em></p>



<p><em><strong>4.&nbsp;Certero for IBM&nbsp;</strong>product does not have any modules&nbsp;</em></p>



<p><em><strong>5.&nbsp;Certero for SAP Applications&nbsp;</strong>product does not have&nbsp;any modules&nbsp;</em></p>



<p><em><strong>6.&nbsp;Certero App-Centre&nbsp;</strong>product is a self-service apps portal/store for desktops&nbsp;with no modules&nbsp;</em></p>



<p><em><strong>7.&nbsp;<a href="https://www.certero.com/certerox/">Certero for Mobile</a>&nbsp;</strong>product covers MDM, MAM&nbsp;with no modules&nbsp;</em></p>



<p><em><strong>8. <a href="https://www.certero.com/saas/">Certero for SaaS </a></strong>has twelve optional modules that cover AWS, AZURE, O365, Salesforce, Adobe CC, Google Workspace, Okta, Wrike, Box, Zoom, Tableau, Dropbox, Slack  </em></p>



<p><em><strong>9.&nbsp;Certero Passworks&nbsp;</strong>product is a self-service password reset solution for Window/Macs with no modules&nbsp;</em></p>



<p><em><strong>10.&nbsp;Certero PowerStudio&nbsp;</strong>product is a PC Power Management solution for desktops&nbsp;with no modules&nbsp;</em></p>



<p>5.&nbsp;<strong>What Gartner said about Certero<em>:&nbsp;</em></strong>Certero declined to participate in the research process for this Magic Quadrant; it identified no reference customers and chose not to provide supplementary information.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>



<p><em><strong>Certero says:&nbsp;</strong>Yes, this is&nbsp;correct.&nbsp;Gartner&nbsp;also informed Certero that it would not be included in the Magic Quadrant.&nbsp;&nbsp;Certero was included in the Magic Quadrant?&nbsp;</em></p>



<p>6.&nbsp;<strong>What Gartner said about Certero<em>:&nbsp;</em></strong>Gartner’s analysis of Certero in this Magic Quadrant is therefore based on other credible sources,&nbsp;including previous vendor briefings, customer inquiries, Gartner Peer Insights reviews and other publicly available information.&nbsp;</p>



<p><strong><em>Certero says:&nbsp;</em></strong><em>Gartner have consistently been unable to&nbsp;move past their internal perceptions, thus why their analysis, understanding or representation of Certero is so misleading. It is the sole reason&nbsp;why Certero refuses to participate. This Magic Quadrant is just another example of how&nbsp;inaccurate the analysis is regarding&nbsp;Certero.&nbsp;A quick review of the website could have corrected many of the inaccuracies in Gartner analysis.&nbsp;</em></p>



<p>7.&nbsp;<strong>What Gartner said about Certero<em>:&nbsp;</em></strong>Gartner Peer Insights indicate that the Certero support team is very responsive and well-versed in SAM, which has led to a high level of customer satisfaction&nbsp;</p>



<p><strong><em>Certero says:&nbsp;</em></strong><em>Yes, this is&nbsp;correct. That’s why In 2019 Certero was the highest rated SAM tools vendor, awarded the Gartner Peer Insights Customers’ Choice Distinction for Software Asset Management tools. A recognition we are very proud of because it was driven by our customers, the people who matter most to us. (<a href="https://www.certero.com/case-studies/">Read the success stories</a>)</em></p>



<p>8.&nbsp;<strong>What Gartner said about Certero<em>:&nbsp;</em></strong>Certero offers a suite of products that extends beyond software asset management, and may be appealing to organizations that wish to manage hardware, software and cloud assets on a single platform&nbsp;</p>



<p><strong><em>Certero says:&nbsp;</em></strong><em>Certero have two products that are not related to IT Asset Management, they are Certero Passworks&nbsp;and Certero PowerStudio.&nbsp;The other eight products are related to IT Asset Management with scope covering from Mobile to Mainframe to Cloud.</em></p>



<p>9.&nbsp;<strong>What Gartner said about Certero<em>:&nbsp;</em></strong>Recently established partnerships with large channel partners within North America have improved its availability within the region.</p>



<p><strong><em>Certero says:&nbsp;</em></strong><em>Certero’s does have partners, one global partner of Certero is the largest in the USA and globally. Availability remains unchanged in all three sub-hemispheres we operate in.&nbsp;</em></p>



<p>10.&nbsp;&nbsp;<strong>What Gartner said about Certero<em>:&nbsp;</em></strong><em>Certero lacks brand awareness due to limited marketing and sales functions, which inhibits its ability to capitalize on new market opportunities.</em><strong><em>&nbsp;</em></strong></p>



<p><strong><em>Certero says:&nbsp;</em></strong><em>Certero does lack brand awareness due to a period of&nbsp;low&nbsp;Sales &amp; Marketing activity&nbsp;regarding IT Asset Management&nbsp;from 2013 – 2017&nbsp;whilst it developed its new technology stack&nbsp;and&nbsp;current product set&nbsp;for IT Asset Management.&nbsp;&nbsp;</em></p>



<p>11.&nbsp;&nbsp;<strong>What Gartner said about Certero<em>:</em></strong><em>Certero primarily operates within Europe, and more specifically within the UK. <p><strong><em>Certero says:&nbsp;</em></strong><em>Certero has more customers in Malaysia than it does in Europe. Certero has more customers in North America than it does in Europe. Certero has more customers in Australia and New Zealand than it does in Europe.&nbsp;</em></p>
